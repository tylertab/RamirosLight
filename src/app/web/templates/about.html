About Trackeo

Built in Atlanta and powered by federations across Latin America.

Mission

We amplify athletes with trusted data, multilingual coverage, and equitable access.

Trackeo centralizes competition data for 19 federations while delivering insights in English, Spanish, and Portuguese.

Our platform supports coaches with workload analytics, empowers fans with contextual storytelling, and streamlines compliance for federation staff.

Team & partners

Trackeo collaborates with local statisticians, journalists, and federations throughout the region.

  • Atlanta HQ

    Product, engineering, and partnerships operate from Atlanta, coordinating multilingual coverage.

  • Regional bureaus

    Embedded correspondents in Bogotá, São Paulo, Santiago, and Mexico City file news and verify results.

  • Federation council

    An advisory council of federation leaders ensures Trackeo meets security and accessibility standards.
